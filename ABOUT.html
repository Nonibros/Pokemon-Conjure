About Pokémon Conjure

Latest update: June 28th 2016

Introduction

Pokémon Conjure is a ROM hack of Pokémon Firered Version produced by Hajima and Nonibros.

Storyline

This is taken directly from the thread, with no grammer fixes:


Prof. Oak and his group has discovered a new era of Pokémon Evolution, a Pokémon full with tremendous power, a evolution called Conjure Evolution. Conjure means the more you devour, the more you get stronger. You, a 10 year old orphan, wanted to know and exceed the average knowledge of Pokémon. So you became Prof. Oak's Lab Assistant for 8 years, Until it was time that he sent you on a journey to research about Conjure Pokémon. Now, the only way to evolve a Pokémon to a Conjure Pokémon, you must get the gem stone far away guarded by Ancient Pokémon. The information soon got into the wrong hands, and have decided to get the stone to use the power to overrun the world. Along your Journey, you meet Kol, your old friend. Kol has secrets, secrets that you want to know, the power of Conjure. You can't make any Pokémon activate the Conjure Evolution, there's a specific way. You need to catch OTHER TRAINER's Pokémon, or find ??? who can tell you how without capturing others. You will be given the ability to control Conjure Pokémon when you obtain the gem stone.
